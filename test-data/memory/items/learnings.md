### 2026-02-14: Executive buyers require recurring KPI narrative
**Source**: QBR - Acme Corp
**Insight**: One-time metric snapshots are less persuasive than recurring KPI trend narrative
**Implications**: Build monthly digest process into renewal motion
**Applied To**: renewal planning, customer communication cadence

### 2026-02-12: Auth fallback increased launch confidence
