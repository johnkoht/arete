# Goals & Strategy

> **Purpose**: Business goals, OKRs, strategic direction, and market positioning.
> **Last Updated**: 2026-02-06
> **Updated By**: Test data

## North Star Metric

**Metric**: Activation rate (users completing onboarding)
**Definition**: % of signups who complete all onboarding steps within 7 days
**Why this metric**: Drives retention and expansion
**Current Value**: 62%
**Target**: 75%

## Strategic Framework

### Vision

Become the default tool for product teams at mid-market companies within 3 years.

### Strategic Pillars

1. **Pillar 1: User Growth**
   - Description: Activation, retention, expansion
   - Why it matters: Foundation for sustainable growth
   - Key initiatives: Onboarding v2, in-app guidance, customer success

2. **Pillar 2: Product Excellence**
   - Description: Quality, usability, support efficiency
   - Why it matters: Reduces churn, enables expansion
   - Key initiatives: Admin setup improvements, support ticket reduction

3. **Pillar 3: Market Expansion**
   - Description: New segments, geographic expansion
   - Why it matters: Long-term TAM growth
   - Key initiatives: Enterprise features, internationalization

## Current Goals & OKRs
