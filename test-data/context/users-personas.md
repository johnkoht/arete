# Users & Personas

## Primary Persona: Product Manager

- Owns roadmap and execution cadence
- Needs fast access to trustworthy context before meetings and planning

## Secondary Persona: Engineering Lead

- Needs clear decisions, priorities, and dependencies
- Uses context to de-risk delivery planning

## Customer Personas

- Buyer (operations leader): cares about activation and ROI
- Champion (product lead): cares about team adoption and outcomes
- Decision-maker (executive): cares about renewal confidence and strategic fit

## Common Pain Points

- Repeating the same discovery context across meetings
- Missing rationale behind earlier product decisions
- Inconsistent prep quality for customer-facing conversations
