# Activity Log

> **Purpose**: Chronological record of significant PM activities. Helps track what happened and when.

---

## How to Log Activities

Add entries for:
- Projects started or completed
- Major decisions made
- Context files updated
- Significant milestones

Format: `## YYYY-MM-DD` with bullet points for activities

---
