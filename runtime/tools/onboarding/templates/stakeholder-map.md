## Relationship Priority Legend

- **Essential**: Must build strong relationship early (manager, key partners)
- **Important**: Should build relationship during onboarding (cross-functional leads)
- **Valuable**: Good to connect with when possible (peers, experts)
- **Future**: Not urgent now, but will matter later

## Relationship Status Legend

- **Strong**: Established mutual trust and rapport
- **Building**: Had meaningful interactions, growing
- **Initial**: Met once, need to deepen
- **Scheduled**: Meeting planned
- **Not yet**: Haven't connected
